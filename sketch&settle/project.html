HERE ARE THE RECENT PROJECTS THAT WE DID

At Sketch and Settle, every project is a story of creativity and precision.
We work hand-in-hand with our clients to design homes that are not only functional but also breathtaking in beauty. From modern architectural marvels to timeless classics,
our portfolio showcases a variety of styles tailored to each client’s needs. Take a look at some of the most recent projects that have brought our vision to life.

Triangled House in Belgium

House of tourists

This unique architectural marvel features a striking triangular frame, offering panoramic views of the Belgian countryside. Designed for travelers, it’s a perfect blend of art and comfort.

Horizon Hill House

Where you'll see beauty of the hills in your hotel.

Built on elevated terrain, this project ensures guests enjoy breathtaking sunrises and sunsets over rolling hills. It combines luxury hospitality with a deep connection to nature.

Bloom Heights

Overseas views and designs

With its open terraces and modern glass structures, Bloom Heights offers a peaceful space to soak in ocean views. Every angle was crafted to maximize light and sea breeze flow.

Seaside Villa

Relax with waves at your doorstep.

This villa embraces coastal living, with floor-to-ceiling windows that bring the sea indoors. Perfect for relaxation, it’s a tranquil escape just steps from the shoreline.

City Lights Loft

Modern living in the heart of the city.

Located in a vibrant urban setting, this loft boasts sleek interiors and large windows framing the dazzling city skyline. It’s designed for those who thrive in the fast-paced city life.

Forest Hideout

Peaceful escape surrounded by nature.

Nestled deep within the forest, this hideout offers a serene retreat. The wooden structure and earthy tones make it blend seamlessly into its lush surroundings.
